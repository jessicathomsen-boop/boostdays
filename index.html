Mission Brief

Be bold.
Build together.
Become AI native.

What would you build if you had two days, a crew of sharp coworkers, and permission to aim higher? That's Boost Days — 15 hours a month, open to everyone from IC2 to leadership. The skills we learn here aren't extra. They're the fuel.

At e-conomic, we are in the AI era and we are all embracing the unknown. Back when humanity went to the moon, it was a shared human project. Thousands of people with different skills working together — backed by radical, infectious optimism.

In times that feel uncertain, that kind of shared exploration isn't just productive — it's a breath of fresh air. It reminds us of what we can build together. That's the spirit we channel.
